# 🌟 Notable Manim Creators

A list of YouTube channels and creators who push the boundaries of Manim.

## 🏆 The Legends
- [3Blue1Brown](https://www.youtube.com/c/3blue1brown) - The creator of Manim. Focuses on the beauty of mathematics.
- [Manim Community](https://www.youtube.com/c/ManimCommunity) - The official channel for the community-driven version of Manim.

## 🚀 Amazing Educational Channels
- [Theorem of Beethoven](https://www.youtube.com/c/TheoremofBeethoven) - Exceptional tutorials and complex mathematical visualizations.
- [Reducible](https://www.youtube.com/c/Reducible) - Beautifully explained computer science concepts.
- [Primer](https://www.youtube.com/c/PrimerLearning) - Simulations and evolutionary biology visualizations.
- [Physics with Manim](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=manim+physics) - Various creators focusing on physics simulations.

## 🎨 Creative Explorers
- [Benjamin Hackl](https://www.youtube.com/c/BenjaminHackl) - Advanced techniques and unique visualization styles.
- [Darrin - Math and Science](https://www.youtube.com/c/DarrinMathandScience) - Deep dives into engineering and physics.
